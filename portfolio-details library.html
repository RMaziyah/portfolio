Project Detail

Saga Library is a web development project aimed at providing novel enthusiasts with an immersive online reading experience. The platform hosts a diverse collection of novels spanning various genres, empowering users to browse, read, and engage with captivating stories. With an intuitive reading interface, users can seamlessly navigate chapters and now have the ability to add books to their wishlist. The wishlist feature allows users to submit books they desire to see included in the Saga Library, fostering a sense of community involvement and ensuring the platform continues to expand its novel offerings. The website also includes search functionality, enabling users to quickly find specific novels. Saga Library serves as a testament to my web development skills, combining my passion for novels.
